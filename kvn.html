🏈 Kyle Van Noy Highlights

One of BYU's greatest defenders — watch KVN dominate before the NFL came calling.

Kyle Van Noy at BYU: Watch Van Noy wreck opposing offenses as a Cougar. His explosiveness off the edge, instincts in coverage, and motor made him one of the most complete linebackers to ever wear the royal blue.

🏫 BYU Career

Van Noy played for the Cougars from 2009–2013, becoming one of the program's most decorated defenders and earning national recognition as an elite edge rusher and linebacker.

🏆 NFL Success

Selected by the Detroit Lions in the 2nd round of the 2014 NFL Draft. He went on to win two Super Bowl rings with the New England Patriots, cementing his legacy as an NFL champion.

⚡ Playing Style

Known for his versatility, relentless motor, and elite instincts, Van Noy could rush the passer, drop into coverage, and stuff the run — a true three-down linebacker at the highest level.

🎓 BYU Legacy

Van Noy is a model for what a BYU defender can become. His film at BYU shows the exact qualities — technique, effort, football IQ — that translate from college to the NFL.
